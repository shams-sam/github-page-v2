A research scientist by day and saving the city by night, I work full-time for a California based startup called Foundation AI. I am currently working on the problem of document analysis trying to solve problems around OCR, effect of gradient illumination and variable orientation in the scanned documents or camera pictures, and rebuilding document structure based on visual aspects of an image.

Prior to this, I worked at Practo, where I tackled problems in NLP and Computer Vision to build intelligent healthcare systems driven by machine learning models. I have built systems from ground up for retrieving clinical concepts from free text leveraging NLP techniques and allocating appropriate ICD-10 code using Dense-LSTM Hybrid DNNs.

I am an alumni from Class of '15, Department of Electrical and Electronics Engineering, NITK Surathkal, India.

This website is built and maintained by me personally. Any improvements or suggestions are welcome.
